₹91 Cr for Lonar Lake Tourism & UNESCO Push

Maharashtra has approved ₹91.29 crore to develop the Lonar Crater Lake in Buldhana district—upgrading infrastructure, memorials, and pursuing UNESCO status. Lonar Lake, also known as Lonar crater, is a saline, soda lake, located at Lonar, 79 km from Buldhana city in Buldhana district, Maharashtra, India. It is a notified National Geo-heritage Monument.
(Times of India)

About the Organization

Paryatan365 is a dedicated tourism platform that celebrates the rich heritage, culture, and natural beauty of Maharashtra. From forts and festivals to beaches and backwaters, we guide you through authentic experiences while promoting sustainable travel.

We bring local stories, traditions, and offbeat places to the forefront — connecting travelers with meaningful journeys and locals with new opportunities. Every step with us is a step toward responsible discovery and cultural appreciation.

Authentic Experiences Local Insights Heritage Tourism

Our Mission

To inspire, educate, and empower travelers to explore Maharashtra responsibly — while preserving its diverse traditions, ecology, and heritage for future generations.

  • ⭐ Promote eco-conscious travel
  • ⭐ Support local artisans and communities
  • ⭐ Encourage cultural exchange and understanding
  • ⭐ Foster heritage conservation awareness
  • ⭐ Provide educational travel experiences
  • ⭐ Bridge urban and rural tourism opportunities

Our History

Founded in 2023, Paryatan365 began as a passion project and has grown into a trusted travel companion for thousands across Maharashtra. From small village roots to a statewide movement, our platform has evolved through stories, collaborations, and the support of enthusiastic explorers like you. Over the years, we’ve partnered with local guides, historians, and cultural experts to enrich our content and tours. Our journey is driven by the belief that travel should not only delight but also educate and empower. Through continuous innovation and feedback from our growing community, Paryatan365 remains committed to delivering authentic experiences that celebrate Maharashtra's rich legacy.

Meet the Founder

Shubham Gaikwad

“Paryatan365 is not just a travel platform — it's a tribute to the soul of Maharashtra.”

As a visionary founder deeply connected to his roots, Shubham Gaikwad established Paryatan365 as a heartfelt tribute to Maharashtra and its vibrant tourism ecosystem. His mission goes beyond promoting destinations — it's about honoring the state's cultural legacy, empowering local communities, and reimagining travel as a tool for unity, sustainability, and pride. With Paryatan365, he envisions a platform that bridges tradition and technology to inspire responsible exploration.
